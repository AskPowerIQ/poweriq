    Website Purpose

    PowerIQ is a premium online electrical guidance platform focused on helping people understand electrical and solar-related issues before spending money, approving work, or panicking about faults.

    The website must communicate:

    • Trust
    • Professionalism
    • Simplicity
    • Clarity
    • Modern digital convenience
    • Safety-first guidance

    The website is NOT:

    • An electrician company website
    • A hardware store
    • A DIY electrical tutorial platform
    • A marketplace

    PowerIQ exists to provide:

    • Guidance
    • Clarity
    • Explanation
    • Quote understanding
    • Fault understanding
    • Electrical support through WhatsApp

    Core Brand Positioning

    Main Brand Message

    “Smart Electrical Advice”

    PowerIQ helps clients:

    • Understand electrical problems
    • Understand inverter and solar issues
    • Understand electrical quotes
    • Understand electricity bills
    • Reduce confusion before spending money

    The platform supports electricians rather than competing with them.

    Brand Personality

    The brand should feel:

    • Calm
    • Intelligent
    • Professional
    • Premium
    • Clear
    • Helpful
    • Modern
    • Trustworthy

    The communication style should feel:

    • Human
    • Easy to understand
    • Non-aggressive
    • Professional but approachable

    Avoid:

    • Overly technical language
    • Fear-based marketing
    • Cheap sales language
    • Complicated explanations

    Safety Positioning

    PowerIQ must always communicate safely.

    The platform should:

    • Encourage professional inspections where needed
    • Avoid dangerous electrical instructions
    • Avoid telling users to open DB boards
    • Limit guidance to basic safe checks
    • Use careful wording

    The platform should never sound like:

    • An official inspection report
    • A licensed compliance certificate
    • Dangerous DIY instruction

    People needing help understanding:

    • Inverter warnings

    • Fault notifications

    Quote Review Clients

    People who:

    • Want a second opinion
    • Feel unsure about pricing
    • Need help understanding scope of work

    International DIY Solar Users

    Users needing:

    • Clarification

    • Safer decision-making

    Purpose:

    Purpose: Protect the business and clarify:

    Purpose: Simple contact methods.

    Main focus:

    • WhatsApp
    • Email
    • Business hours
    • Response expectations
